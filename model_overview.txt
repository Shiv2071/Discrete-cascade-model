Discrete Stochastic Cascade Dynamics — System Overview
=======================================================

A new class of discrete dynamical systems on finite graphs with
irreversible energy depletion and emergent structural hierarchy.

What the system is
------------------
A discrete-time stochastic process on a finite graph. At each vertex:

  X, Y   : counts of two excitation species
            (asymmetry: X-Y and X-X interactions allowed; Y-Y forbidden)
  E(p)   : local capacity energy (finite, strictly non-renewable)
  S(p)   : structural state (accumulates with interactions and bonds)

The system requires no continuum limit, no external drive, and no
infinite-resource assumption.

Update order (one time step)
----------------------------
Interaction (stochastic X-Y and X-X events)
  -> Ripple: F = |S(n) - 2S(n-1) + S(n-2)|
  -> Regime: quiescent (F <= C), leakage (C < F < C+Delta),
             explosive (F >= C+Delta, creates new X-Y pairs)
  -> Bond formation (Landau free-energy functional)
  -> Energy update (irreversible depletion)
  -> Structural state update
  -> Diffusion (conservative; energy does not diffuse)

Proven guarantees (Part I)
--------------------------
1. Energy Monotonicity: total energy is a non-negative supermartingale
   that decreases strictly in expectation whenever the system is active.
2. Finite Activity Bound: cumulative interactions across all space and
   time are almost surely bounded by initial energy / unit cost.
3. Almost Sure Absorption: the system reaches a frozen absorbing
   configuration in finite time with probability one.

Proven necessity (Part II)
--------------------------
1. Symmetric Collapse: identical species => single-species degeneration,
   Landau landscape flattens, bonding phase disappears.
2. Differential Depletion: the forbidden Y-Y channel is independently
   necessary for bond formation.
3. Beat Frequency: frequency mismatch is independently necessary for
   non-trivial dynamics.

Critical threshold: an asymmetry index A exists such that no bonds
form below A_c (sharp critical threshold).
